MapsBridge

What is it?

MapsBridge lets you view your Google My Maps on your iPad. Google My Maps allows you to create custom Google maps marked with your locations. MapsBridge shows you these maps on your iPad. With MapsBridge, your iPad is an atlas, travel planner, real estate guide, and more.
